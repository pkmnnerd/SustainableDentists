By making energy efficiency upgrades, your clinic will use less energy over the long run. This will result in lower energy bills, which means less of your income is spent toward paying energy bills.

Better customer experience

Many of the upgrades will also provide a more pleasing customer experience, making your clinic more attractive. This will bring in many new patients and will get them to keep coming back.
