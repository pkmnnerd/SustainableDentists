About us

We are four students attending the University of Maryland College Park. This site was made for a class in our Scholars program at UMD. The goal of the class is to increase sustainability in the commercial sector in Howard County.

Our Mission

We are a group of students who are very passionate about sustainability and want to see more businesses implementing sustainable practicies. We hope to make inform everyone about how easy it is to make the switch help help people started in the right direction.
